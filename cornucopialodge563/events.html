Regular Gatherings

Stated Meetings

Tuesdays at 7:00 PM

Our regular business meetings where we conduct lodge business, recognize achievements, and enjoy fellowship. Dinner served at 6:00 PM.

Social Hour

Informal gathering for members and guests. Great opportunity to meet brothers, ask questions, and enjoy casual conversation.

Members practice ritual work and prepare for degree ceremonies. Open to all members interested in learning or perfecting their proficiency.
