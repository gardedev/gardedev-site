Our Lodge

A Century of Brotherhood

For over 100 years, Cornucopia Lodge 563 has stood as a beacon of fellowship and service in our community. We are a fraternal organization dedicated to personal growth, charitable works, and the bonds of true friendship.

Our members come from all walks of life, united by shared values and a commitment to making our community a better place. Through our charitable foundation, we support local schools, food banks, and families in need.
