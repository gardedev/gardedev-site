FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

Membership begins with an inquiry. Fill out our contact form or attend a social hour to meet our members. We require two members to vouch for you, and you'll go through an interview process before being voted on by the lodge.

Can I rent the lodge hall for an event?

Yes! Our hall is available for rent to members and the community. It accommodates up to 150 guests and includes tables, chairs, and a kitchen. Contact us for availability and pricing.

Are women allowed to join?

Cornucopia Lodge 563 is a men's fraternal organization. However, we have many events that are open to families and the wider community. We also support and collaborate with women's auxiliary organizations.

Do I need to know someone to join?

While having a member recommend you is helpful, it's not required. Attend one of our public events or social hours to meet members who can guide you through the process.

What are the membership dues?

Annual dues vary based on membership type and are kept affordable to ensure accessibility. We also offer discounted rates for young members under 30 and those experiencing financial hardship.

How can I donate to your charitable work?

Donations to the Cornucopia Foundation can be made via check, credit card, or through our online portal. All donations are tax-deductible. Contact us for more information about planned giving and sponsorship opportunities.
